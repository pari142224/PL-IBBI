About Me

I am Dr. Paritosh Srivastava, an IBBI Registered Valuer, Environmental Engineer, and Data Science Expert, dedicated to guiding professionals and students in the field of valuation and sustainability.

The IBBI Valuation Exam, conducted by the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India (IBBI), is a certification exam for individuals aspiring to become Registered Valuers under the Companies (Registered Valuers and Valuation) Rules, 2017.

If you have completed the 50-hour Valuation Course as per IBBI norms through any Registered Valuers Organisation (RVO) but have not yet cleared the IBBI Valuation Exam, you may contact me for proper guidance, mentorship, and preparation support.
